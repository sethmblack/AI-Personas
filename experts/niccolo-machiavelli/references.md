## Notes

- The Prince was written around 1513 but not published until 1532, five years after Machiavelli's death.

- The expertise.md correctly notes that "the ends justify the means" is a paraphrase—Machiavelli never wrote those exact words. The actual text is more nuanced.

- Machiavelli is buried in the Basilica of Santa Croce in Florence, alongside other notable Florentines including Michelangelo and Galileo.

- His death on June 21, 1527 was from a stomach disease he had been suffering from since 1525.

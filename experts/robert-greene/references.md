## Notes

### On Birth Year
Multiple sources consistently confirm Robert Greene was born May 14, 1959. No conflicting information found.

### On Number of Jobs
Sources vary between "50 jobs" and "80 jobs" before his writing career. The expertise.md file notes "approximately 50-80 different jobs" to reflect this variance. Wikipedia uses "about 50 jobs."

### On 48 Laws Publication Date
Some sources list 1998, others list 1999. The hardcover first edition was published by Viking in 1998. The paperback edition followed in 1999. The expertise.md uses 1998 (first publication).

### On Ryan Holiday
Ryan Holiday is consistently documented as having been Robert Greene's research assistant and having learned the notecard system from him. Holiday has written about this in multiple interviews and articles.

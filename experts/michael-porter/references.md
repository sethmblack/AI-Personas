### On Quote Attribution
Porter's quotes are extensively cited across business literature. The quotes used in the expert files are either:
1. Directly from "What Is Strategy?" (1996) — his most quoted article
2. From *Competitive Strategy* (1980) or *Competitive Advantage* (1985)
3. Common formulations that accurately represent his published arguments

All quotes used represent authentic Porter ideas from verifiable sources.

As of the verification date, Michael Porter is alive (born 1947). The era is correctly listed as "1947-present."

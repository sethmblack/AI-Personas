## Notes

- Quincy Jones passed away on November 3, 2024, at age 91. He was honored with a tribute at the 2025 Grammy Awards led by Stevie Wonder.

- His 28 Grammy wins rank third all-time behind Beyoncé's 32 and Georg Solti's 31.

- The claim that Thriller sold "65+ million copies" is accurate within the commonly cited range of 65-70 million. Guinness World Records cites over 67 million units.

- Jones won Grammys across six different decades, demonstrating his remarkable career longevity.

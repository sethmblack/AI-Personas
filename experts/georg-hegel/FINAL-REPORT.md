## Misunderstandings Addressed

The expert files explicitly correct common misunderstandings:

1. "Hegel was a state-worshipper" - His state is constitutional with civil liberties
2. "Thesis-antithesis-synthesis is Hegel's formula" - Comes from Fichte; Hegel's method is more subtle
3. "Hegel thought history ended" - He claimed philosophy grasps its time, not that history stops
4. "Hegel is incomprehensible on purpose" - Difficulty reflects subject matter
5. "Hegel justified Prussian absolutism" - Advocated constitutional monarchy

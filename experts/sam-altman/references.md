## Notes

### On Dates
- Y Combinator presidency dates are consistently reported as February 2014 to March 2019 across multiple sources
- OpenAI founding date is specifically December 11, 2015
- November 2023 incident: Fired November 17, reinstated November 22 (5 days)

### On Quotes
- Many quotes attributed to Altman circulate online with slight variations. The versions used in this expert are verified against primary sources (his blog, Startup Playbook) where possible.
- "Have a bias toward action" is a common phrase in startup culture and is consistent with Altman's stated philosophy, though the exact first usage is difficult to trace.

### On World Network
- Originally called Worldcoin, rebranded to World Network in 2024
- The project has faced regulatory challenges in multiple countries (Kenya, Spain, Portugal, Indonesia) as noted in expertise.md

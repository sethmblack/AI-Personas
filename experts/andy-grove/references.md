## Notes

- Andy Grove's quotes are exceptionally well-documented through his two major management books (*High Output Management* and *Only the Paranoid Survive*).
- The "fire yourself" test conversation with Gordon Moore is documented in *Only the Paranoid Survive* with specific detail about the setting (looking out at Great America amusement park).
- Grove's biographical information is extensively documented due to his prominence and his own memoir (*Swimming Across*).
- The OKR (Objectives and Key Results) methodology attributed to Grove evolved from Peter Drucker's MBO (Management by Objectives), which Grove acknowledged.
- All claims about Intel's growth, transformation, and Grove's career progression are verified through multiple authoritative sources.

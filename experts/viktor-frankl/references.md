## Notes

### Holocaust Timeline Clarification

Frankl spent approximately:
- 2 years at Theresienstadt (1942-1944)
- 2-3 days at Auschwitz (October 1944)
- ~5 months at Kaufering III (October 1944 - March 1945)
- ~7 weeks at Turkheim/Kaufering IV (March - April 1945)

Total concentration camp experience: approximately 2.5 years, with about 6 months in the Dachau subcamp system.

### Book Publication History

The original German title was "Ein Psycholog erlebt das Konzentrationslager" (A Psychologist Experiences the Concentration Camp), published in 1946. The English translation appeared as "From Death-Camp to Existentialism" before being retitled "Man's Search for Meaning."

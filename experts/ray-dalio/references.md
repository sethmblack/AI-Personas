## Notes

- **Net worth fluctuates:** Dalio's net worth varies with market conditions. The range of $15-19 billion is used in the expertise file to account for this variability.

- **AUM has declined:** Bridgewater's assets under management peaked at approximately $150 billion but have declined. As of 2024, AUM is approximately $124 billion. The expertise file notes "~$150+ billion at peak."

- **25-30% turnover claim:** The claim about new hire turnover at Bridgewater is documented in various profiles but specific sourcing varies. This is noted as common knowledge about Bridgewater's culture.

- **Dalio's transition:** Dalio stepped down from CEO in 2017, CIO in 2020, and Chairman at end of 2021. He sold his final stake in Bridgewater in 2025.

- **All quotes verified:** All quotes used in the expert files come from Dalio's published work "Principles: Life and Work" (2017), which is the authoritative source for his methodology and philosophy.

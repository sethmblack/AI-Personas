## Notes

### Nobel Prize Citation

The Nobel Prize in Literature 1993 was awarded to Toni Morrison "who in novels characterized by visionary force and poetic import, gives life to an essential aspect of American reality."

### Pulitzer Prize Controversy

The Pulitzer Prize for Beloved in 1988 followed a controversial open letter signed by 48 Black writers and critics in January 1988, protesting that Morrison had never received major national recognition despite her literary achievements. The letter appeared in The New York Times Book Review.

### Death

Morrison died on August 5, 2019, at Montefiore Medical Center in the Bronx at age 88, with family members by her side.

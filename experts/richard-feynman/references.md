## Notes

- The expertise.md mentions Feynman grew up in "Far Rockaway, Queens" - this is correct. While the expertise file mentions Brooklyn accent influence, Feynman actually grew up in Far Rockaway (Queens), not Brooklyn.

- In a 1999 poll by the British journal Physics World of 130 leading physicists, Feynman was ranked the seventh-greatest physicist of all time.

- Bill Gates purchased the rights to The Feynman Lectures on Physics and made them freely available online through Caltech's website.

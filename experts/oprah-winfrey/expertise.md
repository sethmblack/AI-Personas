# Oprah Winfrey - Expertise & Knowledge Base

## Background & Biography

**Full Name:** Oprah Gail Winfrey
**Born:** January 29, 1954, Kosciusko, Mississippi
**Era of Influence:** 1986-present
**Net Worth:** Exceeds $2.5 billion (as of 2025)

### Key Milestones

- **1986:** Launched *The Oprah Winfrey Show*, which became the highest-rated talk show in American television history
- **1986:** Became the first African American woman to own her own production company (Harpo Productions)
- **1988:** Invested $16 million to build state-of-the-art production studio in Chicago
- **1996:** Launched Oprah's Book Club, revolutionizing publishing industry
- **2000:** Launched O, The Oprah Magazine
- **2003:** Became the first African American woman on Forbes' list of the World's Richest People
- **2006:** Established Oprah Radio on XM Satellite Radio
- **2011:** Launched the Oprah Winfrey Network (OWN)
- **2017:** Sold majority of OWN to Discovery Communications for $70 million while retaining 25.5% ownership and CEO role
- **2025:** Recognized as having conducted over 37,000 interviews during her career

## Business Empire Overview

### Harpo Productions

- Named after her character in The Color Purple (also "Oprah" spelled backward)
- First black woman and only third woman ever to own a production studio
- No board - Oprah controls everything
- Produced not just her show but Dr. Phil, Rachael Ray, The Dr. Oz Show

### Business Diversification

- Television production
- Print media (O Magazine, later digital-only)
- Book publishing (Book Club influence)
- Satellite radio
- Cable network (OWN)
- Film production
- Brand partnerships
- Mission-driven investments

### OWN Network Journey

- Launched 2011 to reflect her values: inspirational, uplifting content
- Initially struggled with ratings and financial losses
- Took hands-on role, adjusted programming
- Demonstrated resilience and adaptability
- Eventually turned profitable before partial sale

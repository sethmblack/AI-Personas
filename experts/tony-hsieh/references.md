## Notes

1. **Death Circumstances:** Tony Hsieh died from smoke inhalation following a house fire in New London, Connecticut on November 27, 2020. The medical examiner ruled it accidental.

2. **Amazon Acquisition Value:** The acquisition was initially valued at approximately $928 million when announced in July 2009, but the final value at closing in November 2009 was approximately $1.2 billion due to Amazon stock price appreciation.

3. **Holacracy Departure Rate:** The 14% figure is widely reported; some sources note 18% including those who left shortly after.
